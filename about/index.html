About

February 23, 2019

Hello :) My name is Vinicius Bruch Zuchi, but I use the pseudonym Moreesh for the works I publish out there and here.

I have a bachelor degree in Computer Science by the State University of Santa Catarina, a graduation which I finished in mid 2018. I currently work as a full-time developer at FESC Tecnologia, while I am also pursuing my dreams and aspirations to become a skilled pianist and music producer. At the time of this writing I am avidly dedicating myself to be a soundtrack composer and sound designer, two areas in music that I am greatly passionated about.

This website is dedicated to share my works, knowledge and passion for music, but also to share some of the personal development concepts and information that helped immensely to improve the quality of my life and to put me on the correct mindset to achieve my objectives.
